Sponsor or Donate to the Orchestra

The North Queensland Ensembles Inc is a tax deductible fund listed on the Register of Cultural Organisations under Subdivision 30-B of the Income tax Assessment Act 1997. Donations of $2.00 or more are tax deductible. Help us continue to provide quality entertainment for the Townsville community by supporting the orchestra.
